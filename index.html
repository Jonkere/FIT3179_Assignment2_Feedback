Magpies and Swooping in Australia


As we can see, there is an abundance of magpies in Australia.
Now magpies are a fairly aggressive species, which leads us to find that in Australia, there is also an abundance of injuries resulting from magpies swooping.


We see that most of the people that get swooped are cyclists, followed by people playing or engaging in recreation.
So we can see that if you don't want to get swooped by a magpie, then be slow and careful: whizzing down the road on a bicycle is a lot more likely to get you swooped than walking calmly down the road.
